Please Note

  1. This email goes to the webmaster of StephenKing.com, not to Stephen King.
  2. We do not handle interview requests or questions about the rights to Stephen King's work through this website.
  3. The webmaster will not give out Stephen King's email address, home address, phone number, agents' contact information nor any other personal information.
  4. If you are reporting a broken link, please include the page the link was on (e.g. https://stephenking.com/faq) and which link was broken.
  5. If you are reporting a typographical error (typo), please include the page the typo was on (e.g. https://stephenking.com/works/novel/), the word(s) which were misspelled and the approximate location of the typo on the page.
  6. If you are reporting a technical issue, please include the page upon which the issue arose (e.g. https://stephenking.com/faq) and what you were doing when the problem occurred.
  7. If you are contacting us regarding the newsletter, please include the email address with which you signed up.
  8. If your question has already been answered in the FAQ, you will not receive a response.
  9. If your question has to do with The Stephen King Library, please contact them through their website. We are not involved in their day-to-day operations.
  10. All messages are limited to 2048 characters or fewer (including spaces, punctuation, etc.).

Thank you.
